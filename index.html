Door three · What the numbers mean

Reading the frequencies

A frequency here isn't a diagnosis. It's shorthand for where your system is sitting when you walk in — running hot, running low, or somewhere near the middle. You start where you actually are, and the tone walks you toward centre. Nobody starts at centre.

290 – 560 Hz Near centre

Where most people's chest answers back. Around 400 is the common landing place, but yours is yours — it might be 340, it might be 470. A session that starts here is maintenance, not repair.

560 – 900 Hz Running hot

Wired, spinning, stress sitting in the chest, thoughts that won't line up. Also where big abundant energy sits — hot isn't always bad. The tone starts up here and walks down, so you're led rather than forced.
