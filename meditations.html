Guided sessions

Sit down. Breathe with it.

Each session runs the same cadence — four in through the nose, seven held, eight out as a hum. Follow the picture and the voice. Nothing to count on your own.

Before you start

  • Never practice while driving, operating machinery, or in or near water. Breath-holding can cause lightheadedness without warning.
  • Sit or lie down. Don't stand.
  • If you feel dizzy, drop the hold — breathe four in, four out, and stop for the day.
  • Talk to your doctor first if you are pregnant, or if you have a cardiovascular condition, a respiratory condition, epilepsy, or high blood pressure.
  • This is a wellness practice, not medical treatment. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure anything, and it is not a substitute for care from a qualified clinician.
