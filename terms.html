Practice safely

The practice on this site involves slow breathing and holding your breath. That can make you lightheaded, sometimes without warning.

  • Never practice while driving, operating machinery, or in or near water.
  • Sit or lie down before you begin. Do not stand.
  • If you feel dizzy, stop the hold. Breathe normally and stop for the day.
  • Talk to your doctor first if you are pregnant, or if you have a cardiovascular condition, a respiratory condition, epilepsy, or high blood pressure.
  • This practice is for adults. If you are under 18, use it only with a parent or guardian.
